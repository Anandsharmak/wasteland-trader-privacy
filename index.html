Privacy Policy for Wasteland Trader

Last Updated: December 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Wasteland Trader ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile game application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you use Wasteland Trader, certain information may be automatically collected:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Game progress, gameplay statistics, and app performance data
  • Advertising Data: Information collected by third-party advertising services (see Third-Party Services below)

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Payment information
  • Location data
  • Contacts or other personal files from your device

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Our App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information about your device and usage patterns to provide personalized ads. This includes:

  • Device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • Advertising identifiers

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our App, please visit:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's advertising settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the App's functionality
  • Display advertisements through AdMob
  • Analyze app performance and fix bugs
  • Ensure the security and stability of the App

Data Storage

Game progress and save data are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your game data on external servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share aggregated, anonymized data with:

  • Google AdMob: For advertising purposes (as described above)
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the App (analytics, crash reporting)

Children's Privacy

Wasteland Trader is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request information about data we may have collected
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out: Opt out of personalized advertising (see Third-Party Services section)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
